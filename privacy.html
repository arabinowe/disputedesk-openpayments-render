Privacy Policy

DisputeDesk collects the minimum information needed to operate the software business: lead emails, customer emails from Stripe webhook events, feedback, launch settings, and dispute records that users enter or import.

Customer Workspace Storage

After paid access is verified, dispute records may be saved as server-side workspace snapshots encrypted at rest. Do not enter card numbers, bank data, passwords, SSNs, identity documents, or other unnecessary sensitive data into the workspace.

Card Data

Do not enter card numbers into DisputeDesk. Customer checkout and operator ad billing should happen directly inside Stripe, Google, Meta, or Reddit billing pages.

AI Processing

DisputeDesk is AI-assisted software built with Codex from OpenAI. Do not enter sensitive customer evidence unless you have the right to use it and you are comfortable using it in an AI-assisted workflow.

Tracking

The webapp can use Google, Meta, and Reddit tracking IDs that you configure. Use these tools only with appropriate disclosure and consent for your target markets.

Data Access

Use CSV exports and server records to review, correct, or delete records. Keep evidence only as long as needed for customer support and operational purposes.
