Positioning

Lower-cost operations software for merchants. Not a law firm, payment processor, bank, card network, or decision maker.

Money handling

Stripe processes customer payments and pays out to the Chase Business account for AR Trading. DisputeDesk does not transmit, custody, lend, pool, or escrow funds.

Advertising

Use evidence-backed claims only. Avoid guaranteed win rates, guaranteed income, fake reviews, or unsupported AI performance claims.

Email

Commercial email needs accurate sender details, an ad disclosure where appropriate, a postal address, and an opt-out path.

Use at own risk
